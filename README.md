## 🏢 About

Clemens Electric is a locally owned and operated electrical contractor based in **Averill Park, NY**. We deliver trusted, high-quality electrical solutions for residential and commercial customers throughout the Capital District and greater Upstate New York — from small service work to large-scale construction projects.
